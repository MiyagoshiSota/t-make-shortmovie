What to know the difference between “used to” and “To be used to”? I gotchu! Used to: When you are talking about an action or situation that was always done in the past, BUT NOT anymore. EX: I used to smoke, but not anymore.  (You used to always smoke, but now you don’t.) To be Used to: To be familiar or comfortable with a situation or activity based on past experience. Since you did it so many times, it doesn’t bother you anymore. EX: I am used to waking up early. (I started waking up early two years ago, so now it is easy for me.)
